Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the costs? +
    Our service costs vary depending on the plan you choose. For more details, please visit our pricing page.
  • How do I login? +
    You can log in by clicking the "Login" button at the top right corner of the homepage and entering your credentials.
  • How do I create an account? +
    To create an account, click on "Sign Up" and fill out the required information.
  • How do I change my template? +
    You can change your template by going to the "Templates" section and selecting a new design.
  • How can I edit my resume or cover letter? +
    You can edit your resume or cover letter by navigating to the "My Documents" section.
  • How do I change the language? +
    To change the language, go to the settings and select your preferred language from the dropdown menu.
